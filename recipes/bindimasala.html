Bindi Masala

I posted a video for binidi a few months ago and I just felt like my favorite dish deserved more. I think these 5 tips are truly so much more important than the actual recipe, because I know everyone makes it a different way, but I have the receipe below as well!

Ingredients

  • 1lb of okra / bindi, cut in round bite sized pieces
  • 1 small red onion
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 3 green chillies
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder

Instructions

  1. Add cumin seeds to half a tbsp of hot oil in cast iron pan.
  2. Once the seeds start "dancing", add onions and green chilies. Saute on low-medium heat.
  3. After about a minute, add bindi, tumeric, coriander, and red chili powder. Mix well (but gently) and leave on medium-high heat until you get a nice char. Mix to get the other sides occasionally.
  4. Add a sprinkle of salt, tomatoes, mix well, and cover with a lid for approx. 4 minutes.
  5. Once cooked, you can give it one more gentle mix. Serve with some roti or some dal and rice!
